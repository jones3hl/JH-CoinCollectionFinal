Coin Collecting

A person who collects coins is called a numismatist. You can start out your collection by finding certain currency that's limited edition or apart of collections. Many people start out with the 50 state quarters that each have a unique design representing that state. These were produced between 1999 through 2008 with five new quarters released each year.
