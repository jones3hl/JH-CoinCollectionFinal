Foreign Currency

Once your collection has grown a little bit or you prefer to start out with more exotic items, you could consider foreign bills and coins. Every country produces their money in vastly different ways that can add diversity to your collection. Researching what countries have the coins and bills you're interested in is a great place to start before purchasing anything. Some banknotes even have holographic features or come from places that no longer exist.
